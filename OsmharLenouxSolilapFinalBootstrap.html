ASTRO

ASTRO (아스트로) is a South Korean boy group that currently consists of 5 members: MJ, JinJin, Cha Eunwoo, Moonbin, and Sanha. The group debuted on February 23rd, 2016, under Fantagio Entertainment. On February 28, 2023, it was announced that Rocky would be leaving the group following the expiration of his contract with the company.
